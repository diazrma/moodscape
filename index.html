Moodscape

Your Mood History

Enter a short phrase describing your current mood or feeling, then click "Generate" to create a unique visual and audio experience that reflects your emotions. Alternatively, use one of the preset mood buttons to instantly explore different moods. The system analyzes your input to determine if your mood is positive, negative, or neutral, and adjusts colors, animations, and sounds accordingly. Move your mouse over the animation to dynamically change the audio frequency and particle motion for an interactive experience.
